About the Authors & References

Meet the Authors: Hello everyone! Our names are Marinello Zacharie Antoine S. Murakushi, Katriel Anika D. Carreon, and Erbyn Janelle S. Matibag from 9-Arayat. We created this website to share our shared love for Palawan. We hope that we encourage some of you to go there and have fun! Palawan is a lovely place to spend time with your family and enjoy. With its delightful food and beautiful beaches and mountains, it will surely be an unforgettable trip!
